George Clanton Unveils New Single, ‘Fucking Up My Life,’ With Negative Gemini

Ahead of his headlining tour that kicks off tomorrow (October 8) in Brooklyn, vaporwave legend and 100% Electronica founder George Clanton has released a brand new single, “Fucking Up My Life.” The track from his long-awaited upcoming album features rising artist (and Clanton’s frequent copilot) Negative Gemini and details Clanton’s love-hate relationship with touring — as in he loves it, and touring hates him back.

Sabaton Members Reportedly Owe Hundreds of Thousands of Dollars to Swedish Tax Agency

Sabaton singer Joakim Brodén and bassist Pär Sundström reportedly owe hundreds of thousands of dollars to the Swedish Tax Agency following a discrepancy in their tax reporting concerning their income from tours in the United States from 2016 through 2018.

Considered one of the world’s greatest living guitarists, Brooklyn-based Kaki King brings her extraordinary AV projection-mapped show,

In 2015 Kaki launched The Neck is a Bridge to the Body, a ground-breaking multimedia performance that used her guitar as projection screen to explore the genesis of the instrument and her relationship to it as well as her own origin story. The show toured extensively throughout North America, Europe, Australia, Turkey, Japan and Brazil, marking her first foray into multimedia.
